Advent was came from a latin word "Adventus", which means arrival or coming. It is a season when we prepare for the coming of our Lord. It is the first season of the Liturgical calendar. The advent celebration is both a commemoration of Christ's first coming and an anticipation of his second coming.

The Advent season lasts for four sundays. The four sundays of advent are the season of hope, peace, love and joy. These four sundays were represented by candles placed in an advent wreath. December 2, 2018 was the first sunday of advent which is the season of hope. We celebrate the hope we have in Jesus Christ. December 9, 2018 was the second sunday of advent which is the season of peace. We celebrate the peace we find in Jesus Christ. December 16, 2018 will be the third sunday of advent which is the season of love. We celebrate the love we have in Jesu Christ. And December 23, 2018 will be the last sunday of advent which commemorate the season of joy. We celebrate the joy we find in Jesus Christ. The last sunday is the preparation for the celebration of the birth of Jesus Christ.

In preparation for the birth of Jesus Christ, different traditions are present. During the month of December, San fernando City, La Union always have different events that are inline to the Advent season. Last December 1, 2018, the lighting of lights and giant christmas tree at the city town plaza were launched to the citizens of San Fernando City. But no matter traditions people have, at the end of the day we are all gathered to witness and welcoming the coming of our dear Lord, Jesus Christ.
